The Aircraft

Since December 17th, 1903, aviation has never stopped reaching higher.

If the pioneers of that first flight could see what their innovations led to today, they would be astonished. V2 Jets carefully selects the highest level of aviation technology — pairing the world's finest aircraft with crews who treat every detail as their own.

From nimble turboprops to ultra-long-range flagships, every category below is chosen for safety, performance and the quiet luxury our clients expect.
