— Safety

At the forefront of absolutely everything we do.

With V2, getting you to and from your destination safely is our top priority.

We're as discerning about our aircraft partners as you are about service. That's why our team of experts is continually assessing equipment, staff and aircraft for safety.

Our expertise

V2 Jets carefully selects aircraft providers based on stringent guidelines. Working closely with our safety-auditing partners, Wyvern and ARGUS, the safety of our providers is thoroughly reviewed by our Flight Support Team to ensure the highest standard.

Using V2 Jets' industry expertise, each aircraft is evaluated for a range of key factors including:

  • Operational control of the flight
  • The charter company's safety rating
  • The pilots' experience in the aircraft type
  • The pilots' certification and type ratings
  • The pilots' record of accidents, incidents and enforcement actions
  • The status of current insurance for the aircraft

Our partners

Our team of specialists is always happy to work with your corporate flight department, should you need any additional information or requirements. You can be sure that V2 Jets will recommend only the best and most qualified aircraft and crew for your trip.
